About Us

The Synapses STEM Club of Tanza National Trade School is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting excellence in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Through research, innovation, leadership development, and community engagement, the organization cultivates curiosity, creativity, collaboration, and scientific literacy among learners.

Mission • Vision • Objectives

Mission

To inspire and engage young people in STEM through meaningful learning experiences, innovation, and leadership opportunities.

Vision

To cultivate a community of STEM enthusiasts who embrace curiosity, collaboration, and lifelong learning.

Objectives

  • Promote academic excellence in STEM
  • Support student research and innovation
  • Develop leadership and teamwork
  • Engage in community service
