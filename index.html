About the Club

The Synapses STEM Club of Tanza National Trade School is a student-led organization committed to promoting excellence in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Through research, innovation, leadership development, and community engagement, the club provides meaningful opportunities for students to develop critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and scientific literacy.

Mission • Vision • Objectives

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and engage young people in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Through hands-on experiences and meaningful opportunities, we empower students to become future leaders and innovators.

Vision

To cultivate a community of STEM enthusiasts who embrace curiosity, innovation, collaboration, and lifelong learning while pursuing excellence in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Objectives

  • Promote academic excellence in STEM fields
  • Support student research and innovation
  • Foster teamwork and leadership
  • Engage in outreach and community service
